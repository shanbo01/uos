My Future

The future is something scary for me to think of. I got a job offer at the university as a research assistant for one of my professors, but I am still waiting to hear back from him. I enjoy reading and writing and applied for many content writing positions, but I haven't received any e-mails yet. Whenever I think of the future, I just remember my father's words "Never be afraid of something that only God is handling, just try your best and keep moving forward." I know he is right and this is why I am just trying my best now and hoping for the best.
